About

I am a student at the University of Texas Coding Bootcamp. I like music, coffee, and coding. When I was 16 years old my family started a manufacturing company out of our garage that is still going strong today. Seeing that business come to life and succeed was enriching and educational.

A long-term goal I have is to start a software business that employs a large percentage of individuals on the autism spectrum. I would also like to employ a large percentage of developers over the age of 40. In the meantime, if there is a company that has ideas and projects that appeal to me then I would love to work for them.

I have a B.A. in Psychology from the University of Texas at Austin and an M.A. in Psychology from Stephen F. Austin State University.
